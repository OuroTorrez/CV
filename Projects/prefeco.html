PREFECO Archive

Date

January - July 2022 (6 months)

Team

  • Maximiliano Belmonte
  • Alberto Virrueta

Role

Developer

While paper files are convenient on their own, managing thousands of them in an institution presents significant challenges when it comes to review, maintenance, storage, and retrieval. During a six-month internship with the psychopedagogical department of PREFECO High School, we set out to tackle these issues by developing a multifunctional website.

The goal was to develop a platform where students could upload and access their files, receive appointment updates, read news, and stay in touch with their counselors. Counselors, in turn, would have tools to manage student records, post news, recommend materials, and provide personalized feedback.

The project was successfully completed within the given timeframe, and while it met the department's expectations, it was not published due to internal management decisions.
