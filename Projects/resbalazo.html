Resbalazo

Date

October 2021

Team

  • Andres Trejo Reyna

Made With

  • Unreal Engine
  • Blueprint scripts
  • Mixamo and free textures

Inspired by the TV show Wipe-Out and the video game Fall Guys, Resbalazo is a project developed in Unreal Engine using assets from Mixamo and other free 3D model and texture platforms. The goal of this project was to create a fun experience where players can compete against AI-controlled characters across several increasingly challenging courses.

One of the main challenges was level design. While we drew inspiration from classic obstacles such as the iconic red bouncing balls, we also wanted to maintain originality by designing unique and entertaining obstacles. Each course was pixel designed to ensure a perfect fit for every obstacle and jump.

Implementing AI for computer-controlled characters was another challenging aspect. Using Unreal Engine's behavior trees, we simulated AI competitors that were tough yet enjoyable to race against. The goal was to strike a balance where the AI would be challenging but not unbeatable, ensuring a satisfying experience for players.

The use of Git for version control facilitated remote collaboration and team-wide cooperation, making the development process both challenging and rewarding.
