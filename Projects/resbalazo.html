Date A

October 2021

Role

Multifunctional

Made With

  • Unreal Engine
  • Blueprint scripts
  • Mixamo and free textures

Unreal Engine is one of the most common, versatile and powerful game engines in the industry. During my time on school they aked us to build a series of minigames inspired by the switch Mario Party games. Using Unreal Engine and Blueprints, I crafted these games within a tight timeframe of just two to three weeks. To enhance the visual and interactive elements, I incorporated free assets from various sources, including Mixamo.

One of the standout features of the project was the diversity of minigames, each designed with unique mechanics and objectives to keep players engaged.

During the development, I tackled several challenges, such as optimizing game performance and ensuring seamless integration of various assets. This experience allowed me to refine my problem-solving skills and deepen my understanding of game development principles.
