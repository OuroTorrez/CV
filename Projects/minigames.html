Unreal Engine mini-games

Date And Timeline

  • May 2022
  • 2 - 3 Weeks

Role

Multifunctional

Made With

  • Unreal Engine
  • Blueprint scripts
  • Mixamo and free textures

Unreal Engine is one of the most common, versatile and powerful game engines in the industry. For a school project, I was tasked with creating a series of mini-games inspired by the Mario Party games on the Switch. Using Unreal Engine and Blueprints, I crafted these games within a tight timeframe of three weeks. Incorporating free assets from various sources, including Mixamo, to enhance the visual and interactive elements.

A standout feature of this project was the variety of mini-games, each with unique mechanics and objectives to keep players engaged.

Throughout development, I faced challenges such as optimizing game performance and seamlessly integrating various assets. This experience helped me refine my problem-solving skills and deepen my understanding of game development principles.
